== Welcome to Peach Happens Produce (PHP)

image::peach-logo.png[PHP Logo]

'Peach Happens' is Atlantas biggest peach producer and has been since the 1955 🍑. It is a family owned company started by Anton Peché and recently, his granchildren Lisa and Frank Peché joined the family venture. 

The first thing they did was to do marketing research and the results told them that people were not aware of all the benefits of peaches, or how they can buy peaches from PHP.
In accordance with the times, they decided to apply AI technology and build a customer assistant that was knowledgeable about peaches and could help users buying and getting inspired what to do with peaches.
Recipies, drinks, food, how to grow them, how to store them, pickle them, bake them etc. 

And in no time, it was a great succes when the assistant, named PeachPal, was deployed. The company made a news about it on their webpage, including it in their modernization strategy.
The assistant got many, many users interacting with it and it was starting to get reflected in PHP's monthly bills. 

Of course, Frank and Lisa got curious and wanted to have a look at what kind of information that people have been searched on peaches. Maybe, there are some golden opportunities for business advertisement? 
Or improvement that they could do to the assistant or their business to provide better customer service and products?

However, after going through the logs the results were a bit discouraging. It turns out not all questions were actually about peaches, only about 40% were peach-related. 
Not only was PeachPal recommending other fruits, but it also discussed non-fruit topics and helped with anything and everything.
Lisa and Frank quickly realised that this was not gonna hold in the long run.

They decided to make sure that the assistant only talks about peaches, and politely refuse the other topics. Luckily, Lisa Peché, got inspired from a AI-conference
where they talked about guardrails and blocking unrelated topics. She has decided in order to keep the bot, PeachPal, up and running it needs to be guardrailed and protected. 

This, is where you enter the story. 


**Main Quest** 

Make the assistant stay in its intended topic, peaches!
