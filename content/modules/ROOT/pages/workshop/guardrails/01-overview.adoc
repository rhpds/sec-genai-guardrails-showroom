== Welcome to Peach Happens Produce (PHP)

image::peach-logo.png[PHP Logo]

'Peach Happens' is Atlantas biggest peach producer and has been since the 1955 🍑. It is a family owned company and recently, the granchildren to Anton Peché, have taken over. 

They performed a user research and realized that people were not aware of all the benefits of peaches, or how they can buy peaches from PHP. So they built a customer assistant that is knowledgeable about peaches. 
And in no time, it was a great succes! The assistant got many users and it was reflected in PHP's monthly bills! 

Grandkids got curious and wanted to look at what kind of information that people have been searched on peaches. Maybe, there are some golden opportunities for business advertisement?

And they were shocked with the information that people were searching for! They also get recipes for how to make lemon cake! LEMONS! OUTRAGEOUS!

They decided to make sure that the assistant only talks about peaches, and politely refuse the other topics. One of the grandchildren, Lisa Peché, got inspired from a AI-conference in France
where they talked about guardrails and blocking unrelated topics. She has decided in order to keep the bot, PeachPal, up and running it needs to be guardrailed and protected. 


**Main Quest!**: Make the assistant stay in its intended topic, peaches!
